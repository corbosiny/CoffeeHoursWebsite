Peer to Peer Advice for over Twenty Years!

For over 20 years Coffee Hours with Dr. Brian Evans has offered a dynamic platform for vibrant discussions and connections. Led by Dr. Brian Evans, a distinguished figure in Electrical and Computer Engineering at The University of Texas at Austin, this weekly gathering brings together students and faculty in a welcoming atmosphere.

Attendees are invited to engage in open dialogues covering a range of topics, including student life, coursework, career planning, and graduate school preparation. Participants have the opportunity to exchange insights and perspectives with both their peers and experienced faculty members.

Coffee Hours offers great opportunities for academic mentorship. Dr. Brian Evans and the attending alumni offer a wealth of experience and knowledge, ready to support you in your academic journey.

Beyond just advice, Coffee Hours strives to foster a sense of community, enabling attendees to form connections that often extend beyond the event itself. Many have found lasting friendships and valuable mentorships among fellow students and returning alumni.

Whether you're seeking advice or simply a relaxed and engaging Friday afternoon, Coffee Hours with Dr. Brian Evans welcomes you to join a community of inquisitive minds and connect with others who share a passion for learning and dialogue.
